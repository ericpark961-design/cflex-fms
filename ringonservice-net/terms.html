5.1 Inbound Calls

For inbound calls, you must play an automated disclosure such as: "This call may be recorded for quality assurance and compliance purposes." Continued conversation constitutes implicit consent in 1-party consent jurisdictions.

5.2 Outbound Calls

For outbound calls, you must comply with the consent law of the recipient's jurisdiction:

  • 1-party consent states (federal default): automated disclosure recommended at call connect
  • 2-party consent states (CA, FL, IL, MD, MA, MT, NV, NH, PA, WA, DE, CT): explicit recipient consent required before recording

Our platform automatically detects the recipient's area code and applies the corresponding disclosure or stops recording where consent is unavailable.
