5. SMS / A2P 10DLC Disclosures

5.1 Consent (Opt-In)

You will only receive SMS messages from RingOn or our dealer customers after you have provided explicit opt-in consent through one of these means:

  • Texting a keyword (e.g., YES, START) to a published number
  • Submitting a web form with a clear SMS consent checkbox
  • Providing written consent on a paper form during in-person service or vehicle purchase
  • Verbally consenting on a recorded call where applicable

5.2 Message Types and Frequency

Once opted in, you may receive:

  • Service appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Vehicle status updates (ready for pickup, recall notices, financing updates)
  • Account verification codes (one-time passcodes)
  • Customer support replies
  • Optional promotional messages (only when separately opted in)

Frequency varies by program. For the RingOn Operational SMS Alerts program (employee/contractor on-call notifications), the program is limited to up to 30 messages per month. Other consent-based messaging programs (e.g., dealer customer notifications) typically range 2–10 messages per month.

5.3 Opt-Out (Reply STOP)

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying with any of the following keywords:

  • STOP, END, QUIT, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE

You will receive one final confirmation message and will not be contacted again unless you opt back in. Opt-out is processed automatically and immediately by our compliance system. You may also opt out via the web at https://ringonservice.net/sms-optout or by emailing support@ringonservice.net.

5.4 Help (Reply HELP)

Reply HELP to any SMS message to receive sender identification and support contact information, or email support@ringonservice.net.

5.5 Consent Is Not a Condition

SMS enrollment is voluntary. For the RingOn Operational SMS Alerts program, consent is not a condition of employment, contracting, payment of wages or fees, on-call rotation assignment, or access to any RingOn service. Recipients who do not opt in continue to receive operational alerts through email and the internal incident dashboard. Full program terms: https://ringonservice.net/terms-and-conditions.

5.6 Charges

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. RingOn does not charge separately for SMS messages.

5.7 Carrier Disclaimer

Wireless carriers (including T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon, US Cellular, and others) are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is not guaranteed in areas of poor or no signal coverage.

6. How We Share Information

We share information only as described below:

  • Service providers: messaging carriers and aggregators (Twilio, Meta, LINE, Microsoft) strictly for delivery
  • Compliance and legal: when required by subpoena, court order, regulatory authority, or to enforce our terms

We never sell, rent, or share your mobile information, phone number, opt-in data, or messaging content with third parties for their marketing purposes. This includes advertising networks, data brokers, and affiliate marketers.

7. Data Retention

  • SMS / messaging archives required for financial dealer compliance: 5 years minimum, stored on Azure Immutable (WORM) Blob Storage
  • Standard messaging records: 3 years
  • Account information: until account closure plus 90 days, unless longer retention is required by law
  • Opt-out records: retained indefinitely to honor your opt-out preference

8. Data Security

RingOn implements technical and organizational safeguards aligned with the FTC Safeguards Rule:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) with audit logs
  • Multi-factor authentication for staff accounts
  • Incident response plan and 30-day breach notification
  • Annual third-party security review (working toward SOC 2 Type II)
