Program Description

RingOn Operational SMS Alerts

By enrolling, you will receive SMS messages from RingOn Service Corp covering the following operational categories:

  • UPS power and battery alerts from monitored client sites
  • Network device and switch outage alerts
  • Microsoft Teams Phone and SBC service incidents
  • Data-center facility alarms (temperature, power, environmental)
  • Scheduled maintenance window confirmations
Message Frequency Up to 30 messages per month
Message & Data Rates Msg & data rates may apply
Supported Carriers Compatible with all major US carriers (AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, US Cellular, and others)
⚠Important — Enrollment is Voluntary

Your enrollment in RingOn's operational SMS alert program is ENTIRELY VOLUNTARY and is NOT A CONDITION of:

  • Your employment with RingOn Service Corp.
  • Any contracting or sub-contracting relationship
  • Payment of wages, fees, or compensation
  • On-call rotation assignment
  • Access to any RingOn service, system, or facility

If you choose not to opt in, you will continue to receive operational alerts via email and the internal incident dashboard. There is no penalty, reduction in service, or change in employment status for declining or later opting out.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS alerts at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any RingOn SMS message
  • Visiting https://ringonservice.net/sms-optout
  • Emailing support@ringonservice.net

Opting out is immediate and free. After opting out, you will continue to receive operational alerts via email and the internal incident dashboard.
